# Lumina Beauty — Campaign Moodboard

**Brand personality:** clean, intentional, science-backed, softly luxurious, anti-excess

```
Brand:                 Lumina Beauty
Personality:           "quiet luxury, ingredient-first, honest, modern-minimal"
Color language:        cream, warm white, soft sage, pale terracotta — avoid bright/saturated
Avoid:                 clinical/pharmaceutical aesthetic, overly glossy, busy compositions
```

---

## Direction 1: Raw & Honest

**Visual language:** Unretouched textures, natural imperfections, morning light. The campaign lives in real kitchens and bathrooms — not studios. Surfaces show grain, shadow, and warmth.

**Mood:** Intimate, real, quietly confident

**References:** Aesop campaign photography (2022), Glossier "You Look Good" series, Jamie Beck slow photography

**Why it works for Lumina:** Conscious consumers distrust over-production. This direction signals authenticity and ingredient honesty without saying either word.

### Direction 1 — Prompt Pack

**Prompt 1 (Hero — Instagram Feed 4:5):**
```
skincare serum bottle on aged linen cloth next to a half-eaten piece of sourdough toast, morning kitchen light, editorial documentary photography, soft warm window light, 35mm lens slight low angle, intimate and real, warm cream tones, grain texture --ar 4:5 --style raw --no watermarks, text, studio look, harsh shadows, oversaturated
```

**Prompt 2 (Lifestyle — Stories 9:16):**
```
woman in her early 30s applying skincare serum at bathroom mirror, morning light, authentic documentary photography, soft diffused natural window light, 35mm lens handheld slight shake, unhurried and real, muted warm tones --ar 9:16 --style raw --no stock photo pose, fake smile, overly polished, studio lighting
```

**Prompt 3 (Product Close-up):**
```
glass serum bottle on rough linen surface, macro editorial photography, raking side light showing texture and shadow, 90mm macro lens extreme close-up, raw and tactile, warm neutral palette --ar 1:1 --style raw --no watermarks, text, clean white background

**Prompt 4 (Ingredient Story):**
```

## Direction 2: Clean & Considered

**Visual language:** Studio minimalism elevated. Tight compositions, architectural lighting, negative space used as a design element. Every prop is intentional — one botanical, one texture, nothing more.

**Mood:** Precise, serene, confident

**References:** Byredo campaign imagery, Aesop product photography, Muji brand aesthetic

**Why it works for Lumina:** Reinforces "ingredient-first" positioning through visual restraint. The quietness of the image communicates premium without ostentatious styling.

## Direction 3: Soft Science

**Visual language:** The clinical translated to beautiful. Petri dish aesthetics, ingredient magnification, laboratory-inspired but warm. Science as artistry.

**Mood:** Curious, modern, intelligent

**References:** Drunk Elephant campaign (ingredient focus), Paula's Choice editorial, Tata Harper botanical photography

**Why it works for Lumina:** Science-backed positioning visualised without feeling pharmaceutical. Appeals to the "research the ingredient list" consumer who is Lumina's core buyer.
